Hi!

I am a Solutions Architect in Gen-AI at Nvidia. Previously I was a founding machine learning engineer at Lamini AI where I created a software stack for enterprise customers to develop and host their custom production-ready LLMs.

My interests and experiences lie in development, productionization, and optimization of AI models, through human-inspired or divergent mechanisms. I completed my Masters from Stanford in the Symbolic Systems program in the summer of 2023, where I worked with Prof. Fei-Fei Li on decoding EEG-brain signals for robotic manipulation. I also worked with Prof. Justin Gardner to design deep learning algorithms for modeling areas of the Visual Temporal Cortex on fMRI NSD Dataset. I interned at Google Translate working on scaling laws for neural-based evaluation metrics for machine translation models.

I am passionate about research in theoretical and applied deep learning and NLP. I was an NSF-funded RIPS Scholar at the Institute of Pure and Applied Mathematics at UCLA where I worked with Ads and Privacy teams at Google, LA on devising privacy protection frameworks.

I am passionate about communities and aim to create equal opportunities in STEM by promoting diversity and inclusion. I was a Lead at Women Who Code Delhi and founded the community MLNerdie x Delhi to bridge the gap between academia and industry in machine learning. For my initiatives, I was awarded the Google Women Techmakers Scholarship in 2019.

Relevant Tags: Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Large Language Models, Multimodal systems, Cognitive Neuroscience, Women In STEM
